Founded in 2015, Garg Glasses Co. is India’s premium mirror and glass brand. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create pieces that enhance spaces — from homes and hotels to showrooms and offices.

Our factory uses advanced tempering and polishing technologies. Every mirror is checked for quality, durability, and brilliance before it reaches you.

We don’t just create mirrors — we reflect your vision with clarity, style, and heart.
